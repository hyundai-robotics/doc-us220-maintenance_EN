# 1.7. Safety Labels

To prevent safety accidents that could occur during the use of the robot, safety nameplates, such as the labels shown below, are attached to the manipulator. Do not replace or remove them unnecessarily. Any act of damaging the safety labels, such as nameplates, warning signs, safety symbols, name signs, and wire marks attached to the robot, such as moving their position, painting them, putting a cover on them, etc., is prohibited.
